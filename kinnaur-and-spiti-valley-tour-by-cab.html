Kinnaur and Spiti Valley Tour By Cab

₹ . ON REQUEST

Duration

08 Night 09 Days

Tour Code

  KST3

Location

Kinnaur

Tour Plan

On arrival at Chandigarh Railway station, our representative will pick-up you and transfer you to Sarahan. Full day journey Chandigarh to Sarahan on arrival at Sarahan check in at hotel and overnight stay at Sarahan hotel.

Today morning after breakfast checkout from the hotel and visit Sarahan "Mata Bhimakali Temple" after a drive to Sangla. On the way visit Tranda Dhank and after that drive to Sangla Valley. Evening time you reach Sangla, check in at the hotel and overnight stay at Sangla hotel.

Today morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to visit "Chitkul Last Village Of Hindustan" after visiting Chitkul Valley drive back to Nako Village. Full day journey Chitkul to Nako Village on the way you see Small Villages Like Moorang, Pooh, Khab enjoy the beautiful Valley of Kinnour evening time your reach in Nako check in at hotel and overnight stay at Nako hotel.

Today morning, after breakfast check out from the hotel and first Visit Beautiful "Nako Lake" after that drive to Tabo on arrival at Tabo Village visit "Tabo Monastery" after that drive to "Dhankar Monastery & Village" enjoy the Valley and Monastery. Full day enjoy the sightseeing evening time drive to Kaza, On arrival at Kaza check in at hotel and overnight stay at Kaza hotel.

Return to your hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Today morning after breakfast visit Komik village sightseeing, This village is world’s highest village with a road. Roughly 20 km from Kaza, it is a small, beautiful village located at 18,000 feet above sea level.

Hikkim village, at an altitude of about 4440 Mtrs, is about 4 KMs from Komic and about 16 KMs from Kaza. This makes the entire circuit from Kaza to Langza to Komic to Hikkim to Kaza of about 44 KMs, easily doable at comfort in a day. Hikkim village is popularly known for the highest post office in the world and the highest polling station in the world. Langza Village is a beautiful destination, located in one of the most majestic valleys in India 'Spiti Valley' in the state of Himachal Pradesh. This picturesque village is blessed with ancient monasteries and buildings of the prehistoric era. It is surrounded by green pastures, snow-clad mountains and barren landscapes that awe-struck every person who visit this stunning location, which is certainly one of the best places in the whole Spiti Valley.

The magic of nature at Langza village leaves everyone speechless. Langza village is situated at an altitude of 4400 meters and is divided into two sections, namely Langza Yongma (lower) and Langza Gongma (upper). It houses a population of approximately 137 people, who are dependent on agriculture and business for their livelihood. The production of vessels of all shapes and sizes and crafts such as shawls, carpets, etc. full day enjoy the sightseeing after that drive to hotel and overnight stay at Kaza hotel.

Today morning after breakfast visit Kaza local sightseeing like Key Monastery Key Monastery or Kye Gompa is one of the most popular places to visit in Spiti valley, located in the Kaza region. Overlooking the Spiti River at an altitude of 4112 m, Key Monastery is one of the biggest gompas known for the stunning Buddha shrine.

Kibber is one of the most beautiful villages that you can see in spiti and is located around 16 km away from Kaza town. Located at a height of 4270 m, the village till recent claimed the status of being the highest village in the world connected by motorable road. The road to Kibber village crosses one of the most popular monasteries in the region—Key Monastery.

Chicham Bridge, the highest bridge in Asia, and quite an astounding spectacle at that. The bridge is at a staggering height of 13596 ft and connects the two villages named Chicham after a full day sightseeing drive back to hotel and evening time visit local market and overnight stay at Kaza hotel.

Today morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and drive back from Kaza to Kalpa. On the way visit "National Park Pin Valley and Mud Village" Sightseeing. After a full day journey to Kalpa. Evening time you reach Kalpa, check in at the hotel and overnight stay at Kalpa hotel.

Today morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and drive to visit Kalpa Local Sightseeing Roghi Village, Sucide Point, Kalpa Local Market, Reckong Peo and Kinner Kailash Valley. After that drive back to Narkanda. Full day journey to Narkanda evening time you reach in Narkanda check in at hotel and overnight stay at Narkanda hotel.

Today morning check out from Hotel and proceed to visit "Mata Hatu Peak Temple and Tanu Jubber Lake" after that journey to Chandigarh. On arrival in Chandigarh take a transfer to the desired railway station or airport for your onward journey as per schedule.
