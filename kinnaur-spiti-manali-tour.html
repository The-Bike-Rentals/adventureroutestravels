Tour Plan

On arrival at Chandigarh Railway station, our representative will pick-up you and will transfer to Narkanda (65 km from Shimla) famous for skiing and winter sports. Moving 8 km on the link road bifurcation from Narkanda is the Hatu Peak (3300 m). The highest motor able peak of this region gives the view of entire ranges of Himalayas.

On the top is the Hatu Mata Temple. Moving 6km on Himachal Horticultural Land Kotgarh reaches Tani Jubbar Lake near Jarol. Crossing the apple orchards and having a glance at village houses reach Thanedaar. Riding forward from the famous Stokes Apple farm reach Bitthaal where the road connects to NH22. Move forward to Sarahan. On arrival at Sarahan check in at hotel and overnight stay at Sarahan hotel.

Today morning after breakfast checkout from the hotel and visit Sarahan "Bhimakali Temple" after visit drive to Sangla. On the way visit Tranda Dhank and after that drive to Sangla Valley. Evening time you reach in Sangla check in at hotel and overnight stay at Sangla hotel.

In the morning visit a nearby village Chhitkul(26 km) fully lush green and wide open valley with handful of houses. From here gear up yourself to Kalpa (62 km), crossing the deadliest road and high mountain view, crossing Hydro projects and Tapri reach Reckong Peo. Move 13 km upward to Kalpa (10,900ft.) in front of Kinnar Kailasha. The night stay in Kalpa gives you the serene view of Kinnar Kailasha and the air will energies you to move forward.

Today morning after breakfast proceed with the sight seen of Kalpa visting the locations like Roghi village, Kinner Kailash, Kamru FortSuicide Point. Kalpa can be visited in summer, monsoon or winter after that drive to Tabo on the way visit Ribba, Moorang, Pooh, Khab and Nako Lake. After that drive to Tabo on arrive at Tabo check in at hotel and overnight stay at Tabo hotel.

Today morning after breakfast check out and move towards Crossing Hoorling you see Barren clay color hills with snow on peaks. Tabo Monestry on the left of road after that drive to Pin Valley and Mud Village.

Dhankar Village is one of the most stunning attractions in Lahual and Spiti Valley, a home to the most beautiful and awe-inspiring Dhankar Monastery and Dhankar Fort that are perched on the top of the hill in Dhankar. Dhankar is located in the cold desert of Spiti Valley and looks like a bowl-shaped structure in the hills. It looks almost as if all the old Dhankar houses and fort have emerged straight from the mountains with their back wall being merged on the hill.

Today morning after breakfast visit Komik village sightseeing, This village is world’s highest village with a road. Roughly 20 km from Kaza, it is a small, beautiful village located at 18,000 feet above sea level.

Hikkim village, at an altitude of about 4440 Mtrs, is about 4 KMs from Komic and about 16 KMs from Kaza. This makes the entire circuit from Kaza to Langza to Komic to Hikkim to Kaza of about 44 KMs, easily doable at comfort in a day. Hikkim village is popularly known for the highest post office in the world and the highest polling station in the world. Langza Village is a beautiful destination, located in one of the most majestic valleys in India 'Spiti Valley' in the state of Himachal Pradesh. This picturesque village is blessed with ancient monasteries and buildings of the prehistoric era.

It is surrounded by green pastures, snow-clad mountains and barren landscapes that awe-struck every person who visit this stunning location, which is certainly one of the best places in the whole Spiti Valley. The magic of nature at Langza village leaves everyone speechless. Langza village is situated at an altitude of 4400 meters and is divided into two sections, namely Langza Yongma (lower) and Langza Gongma (upper). It houses a population of approximately 137 people, who are dependent on agriculture and business for their livelihood. The production of vessels of all shapes and sizes and crafts such as shawls, carpets, etc. full day enjoy the sightseeing after that drive to hotel and overnight stay at Kaza hotel.

Today morning after breakfast visit Kaza local sightseeing like Key Monastery Key Monastery or Kye Gompa is one of the most popular places to visit in Spiti valley, located in the Kaza region. Overlooking the Spiti River at an altitude of 4112 m, Key Monastery is one of the biggest gompas known for the stunning Buddha shrine.

Kibber is one of the most beautiful villages that you can see in spiti and is located around 16 km away from Kazatown. Located at a height of 4270 m, the village till recent claimed the status of being the highest village in the world connected by motorable road. The road to Kibber village crosses one of the most popular monasteries in the region—Key Monastery.

Chicham Bridge, the highest bridge in Asia, and quite an astounding spectacle at that. The bridge is at a staggering height of 13596 ft and connects the two villages named Chicham after full day sightseeing drive back to hotel and evening time visit local market and overnight stay at Kaza hotel.

Day begins with the journey towards Kunzam Pass (15,060 ft.) 78 km from Kaza. One of the highest motorable passes across the Kunzam range. Crossing Losar in couple of hours reach Kunzam Pass. Ride down to Batal 30km.The off road track to Chander Taal Lake diverts 5km before Batal. Crossing the countless stream reach Chhatru, where greenery start increasing After reaching Chandartal check in at Camp and overnight stay at Camp.

) Today morning After breakfast check out from camp and and precede your journey to Manali. It will be a journey of 8 Hrs to 9 Hrs. This day we will cover the sight seen of Batal and Rohtang Pass on the way back to Manali. In the evening on arrival in Manali proceed with your check inn at hotel followed by dinner and overnight stay.

Today morning check out from Hotel and proceed with your further journey to Chandigarh. On arrival in Chandigarh take a transfer to the desired railway station or airport for your onward journey as per schedule.
