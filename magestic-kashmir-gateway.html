Tour Plan

Welcome to Jammu Railway Station, Our representative will pick you and drive to Srinagar. On arrival check in to the hotel. Dinner with Overnight stay at Hotel.

Today morning have excursion of Srinagar sightseeing like: Dal lake, Char Chinar and Kabootar ghar, Mughal gardens, Shalimar Garden, Nishat Garden and Cheshmashahi Garden, For those who are devotee of Lord Shiva, a visit to the Shankar Achariya Temple is a must. Once you cover all the places return back to the hotel/House Boat. Overnight stay in the hotel/House Boat.

Today morning check out from the Hotel, start your journey Srinagar to Sonmarg .On the way you can enjoy the Pampore, Awantipora, Awoora. On arrival check in to the hotel. After done all the sightseeing Drive to Kargil . Overnight stay at Kargil Hotel.

Today early mornings after breakfast Kargil Sightseeing Mulbekn Monastery, Drass War Memorial, kargil Main Street. On reaching at Leh, Check in to the Hotel. Dinner & overnight stay at the Hotel.

Today early morning have breakfast and start Leh local sightseeing. You can visit Thiksey Monastery, Stok Palace Leh Palace & Hall of Fame – War Martyrs. Have lunch on route and come back to Leh to witness the sunset at Shanti Stupa. Explore the local Leh market after that return to the hotel. Dinner & Over night stya at the hotel.

Early morning have breakfast in the hotel then check out from the hotel then start your trip the Khardung La Pass, situated at 18,380ft, the world’s highest motorable road, to enter the mystic Nubra Valley. Sandwiched between the Karakoram Range to the north and Ladakh range to the south, lie the Shyok and Nubra Rivers.

Visit the white sand dunes at Hunder, a rare cold desert at this altitude. Ride the unique double-humped Bactrian camels here, the only place in the entire world where they can be found. The day ends with an overnight tent stay under the magnificent night sky full of stars.

Early morning have breakfast check out from the hotel and Driver to Leh via Khardung La Pass. Onarrival check in to the hotel and dinner with stay in the hotel.

Next morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and return back to Sarchu Travel by surface from Leh to Sarchu Tanglang La (5360 m) Lachang La (5065 m) over Barlacha La pass (4,890 m). Once you arrive at Sarchu. Check in to the hotel. Dinner & Overnight stay in the hotel.

Next morning after breakfast check out form the hotel and drive to Manali Via Atal Tunnel, Solang Valley enjoy various thrilling adventure activities like: Zorbing, Paragliding, snow scooter, Zip Lying etc. After that check in to the hotel. Dinner with Night stay in the Manali.

Today morning after breakfast check out from the hotel and we will proceed to enjoy the city tour1. Hadimba Devi Temple.- Manali has many attractions but the chief central of interest, historically and archaeologically, is undoubtedly the dungri temple dedicated to the Goddess Hadimba ,the Wife of Bheema of Mahabharata Fame, This is wooden temple, In the dense forest of magnificent deodar, is about 2.5 Kms from the Mall. It is a pleasant stroll to the temple which was built in 1553 A.D.

1. Vashisht Vashisht(3.5 kms from Mall) Hot Sulphur Water springs - Renowned for its hot water and temples. The springs of Vashisht are believed to possess medicinal properties. The waters of the hot brooks are believed to heal skin diseases. Nearby is the pyramidal stone temple dedicated to vashisht Muni & lord Rama temple is also there.

2. Tibetan-Monastery (0.5 Kms) - This Gadhan Thekchoking Gompa dominates the Tibetan area around the bottom of the Mall in Manali. The Tibetan refugees built the Gompa in the late 1969. The main attraction of the monastery is the handicrafts of Tibetan artistry and the carpets that are woven and sold in the monastery. Club House (2 Kms from Mall) - Located on the left bank of Manalsu Nallah. Club house is famous for a amusement activities like skating, Disco Theque etc. Club house is surrounded by various picnic spots.

Today morning after breakfast, enjoy the Kurif sightseeing visit Kufri , famous for its trekking and hiking trails. Adventure seekers, especially in winters,travel to Kufri to enjoy skiing and tobogganing on its snow-covered slopes. You can also visit Indira Tourist Park from where you enjoy a panoramic view of the locations around. Enjoy a pony or a yakride around the park on direct payment basis or enjoy a walk through the thick cedar woods to Mahasu Peak for an amazing view of the mighty.

Faggu Valley, Green Valley and Mini Zoo is also best places to explore in Kufri, and Return back and visit the Jakhoo In Evening Explore Scandal Point, The Mall, Ridge, Lakkar Bazaar, and Christ Church. Dinner and overnight stay at Hotel.

Today early mornings after breakfast check out from the hotel and proceed to your departure city , the cab will drop you at Chandigarh airport / Railway Station.
